## Meeting the World: Four Essentials

As we step into the world living out our faith through righteous action and good works, we must engage wisely and graciously:

- **Be wise in action** (v. 5) — Our conduct must reflect careful discernment
- **Use every opportunity** (v. 5) — Every encounter is a chance to demonstrate Christ's character
- **Be gracious in words** (v. 6) — Our speech seasoned with salt, full of grace
- **Speak clearly** (v. 4) — Our message about Christ must be articulated with clarity and conviction

## The Critical Connection

Are our good works naturally leading to good words? This is the essential question. When our righteous living doesn't translate into gracious, clear speech about our faith, we've broken the bridge between demonstration and proclamation. We must ask ourselves honestly: What prevents our good works from becoming a doorway for good words?
